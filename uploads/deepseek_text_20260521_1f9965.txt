[OMNISCOPE CONTINUED] ANALYSIS ENGINE CATALOG (EVERY FIELD)

3.6 ENVIRONMENT & AGRI: Satellite/drone NDVI, climate model NetCDF analysis, seismic hypocenter location, water quality, soil spectral analysis.

3.7 HUMANITIES & ARTS: Stylometry, music chord recognition, art pigment spectral matching, legal contract clause extraction.

3.8 GENERAL-PURPOSE: Full R-like statistics, AutoML (XGBoost to Transformers), deep vision/NLP, time series forecasting (Prophet, N-BEATS), causal inference, optimization solvers.
